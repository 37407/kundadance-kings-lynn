KundaDance® Classes in King's Lynn

KundaDance® is a 60-minute, fun, uplifting, cardio and dance workout developed by Maya Fiennes. It is a creative movement class that combines Kundalini Yoga, Tai Chi and Qi Gong, meditation and breathing. It is both energising and calming and brings balance to the body, mind and soul.

KundaDance® classes combine music, movement, and mindful energy work - perfect for beginners and anyone looking for a fun, cardio dance experience. Each session helps you release tension, energize your body, and leave feeling uplifted and balanced.
